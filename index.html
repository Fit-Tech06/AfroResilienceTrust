CO-FOUNDER of Afroresilience Trust: ARTIST BIOGRAPHY

Erinle Anthony Taiwo is a visionary visual artist from Lagos, Nigeria, known for igniting meaningful conversations about identity, resilience, and the human experience. Through his unique blend of charcoal, acrylic, and coffee, Anthony's art challenges societal norms and fosters empathy, shedding light on crucial issues like Vitiligo Awareness and mental health.

As an artist, Anthony draws inspiration from his personal experiences and the struggles of everyday people, using his platform to amplify the voices of those often unheard. He has collaborated with notable brands like ZeeVitiligo Nigeria and is currently under training with Art Class Lagos in collaboration with O'DA Art Gallery.

Recent highlights include his participation in the Kongi Art Exhibition (2024) and Ogun State Live Painting Performance (Abeokuta, 2024), as well as his solo exhibition, "Living Positively with Vitiligo", which debuted on June 28th, 2025.

Through his art, Anthony aims to inspire others, promote understanding, and foster a sense of community. He believes that art has the power to transcend boundaries and connect people on a deeper level, and he invites viewers to join him on this journey of self-discovery and connection.
